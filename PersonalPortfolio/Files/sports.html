A Decade of Dedication, Achievement and Friendships

Ju-Jitsu is a dynamic and versatile martial art that blends elements of traditional Japanese jujutsu with modern techniques. It focuses on self-defense, grappling, joint locks, and throws, combined with striking techniques. As a multifaceted discipline, it emphasizes both mental and physical agility, making it an excellent way to build discipline, confidence, and resilience.

I began my journey in Ju-Jitsu at the age of 7 and quickly developed a passion for the sport. After my first belt test, I began competing in the Duo discipline with my best friend in 2012. We trained intensively and became very successful, eventually earning a spot in the state team of Saxony-Anhalt in 2016. Over the next ten years, Ju-Jitsu not only shaped my physical capabilities but also helped me forge lasting friendships and gain valuable life experiences. Unfortunately, due to physical reasons, I had to step back from competition, but I continued to contribute as a co-trainer, helping teach the next generation of Ju-Jitsu practitioners.
